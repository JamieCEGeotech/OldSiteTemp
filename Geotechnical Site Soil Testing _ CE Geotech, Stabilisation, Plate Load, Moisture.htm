CE Geotech

Cutting Edge Geotechnical

CE Geotech specialise in the assessment and validation of hydraulically bound soils for ground engineering applications.

At CE Geotech we deal with projects. This approach ensures our clients receive the highest level of continuity and project management, all the way through a stabilisation project lifecycle; from initial site characterisation, laboratory mix design study phases, and project sign-off through on-site control and validation testing.

If you would like more information on CE Geotech’s capabilities, please download our capability statement

Site Testing Services

Utilising our expertise and experience gained over many years, we offer a fully integrated materials testing service in all aspects of soil stabilisation works from initial site visit, sampling, trial pitting and DCP testing through to earthworks control and validation testing during the construction phase.

Through years of experience on site, our engineers not only provide timely and robust data for stabilisation control, but can also assist with the interpretation of challenging site conditions.
